Taco Recipe

Calories: 259

Time: 1 hour

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 onion
  • 500 grams minced beef
  • 500 grams shredded chicken
  • 1 Strand of Rosemary
  • 2 Teaspoons of sesame seeds
  • 1 jar Heinz Spicy Nachos sauce
  • 12 tacos

    • Toppings

  • Chopped capsicum
  • Grated cheese
  • Chopped lettuce

Method

Mince:

  • 1. Heat oil in a pan over high heat. Add onion and cook for 2 minutes.
  • 2. Add mince and cook, breaking up with a wooden spoon, until browned.
  • 3. Add Heinz Spicy Nachos sauce stir and bring to the boil.
  • 4. Serve in the tacos with additional toppings.

Chicken:

  • 1. Heat oil in a pan over high heat. Add onion and cook for 2 minutes.
  • 2. Add chicken and cook, breaking up with a wooden spoon, until browned.
  • 3. Add rosemary and crushed sesame seeds.
  • 4. Serve in the tacos with additional toppings.
